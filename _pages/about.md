I am Yongkang Duan, a Ph.D. student in Software Engineering at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, jointly trained with Beijing Zhongguancun Academy. I received my Bachelor's degree in Information Resources Management from Sichuan University and my Master's degree in Information Science from Beijing Normal University. My research focuses on **AI4Social Science**, exploring how artificial intelligence, machine learning, and big data technologies can be applied to address complex problems in social science research, aiming to promote paradigm innovation and methodological breakthroughs in social science research through advanced computational methods and techniques.

我是段永康（Yongkang Duan），目前在北京邮电大学软件工程专业攻读博士学位，同时与北京中关村学院进行联合培养。我本科毕业于四川大学信息资源管理专业，研究生毕业于北京师范大学情报学专业。我的主要研究方向是 **AI4Social Science**（人工智能赋能社会科学），致力于将人工智能、机器学习和大数据技术应用于社会科学研究领域，通过先进的计算方法和技术手段，推动社会科学研究的范式创新和方法突破。
